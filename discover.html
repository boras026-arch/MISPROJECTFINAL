Discover UMD & Duluth

Learn why I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth and what makes this area special to me.

Why I Chose University of Minnesota Duluth

I chose UMD because of its strong business program, welcoming community, and its great location. UMD provides many opportunities through education and personal growth. I appreciated the variety of programs and the supportive atmosphere that UMD is known for.

The Labovitz School of Business and Economics at UMD offers a comprehensive Business Finance program that aligns perfectly with my career goals. The faculty are experienced professionals who bring real-world insights into the classroom, and the program emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical application.

Beyond academics, UMD's campus community is welcoming and inclusive. The smaller class sizes allow for more personalized attention and meaningful relationships with professors and peers. The university's commitment to student success is evident in the resources available, from career services to academic support.

The location in Duluth also played a significant role in my decision. The city offers a unique blend of urban amenities and natural beauty, making it an ideal place to live and study. The proximity to Lake Superior and the surrounding natural areas provides endless opportunities for outdoor recreation, which aligns perfectly with my interests in fishing, hunting, and other outdoor activities.

What I Like About Duluth and the Surrounding Area

Duluth is one of the most interesting places I have been. For me it has the perfect mix of city life as well as nature trails and lakes. I enjoy walking the different parks and trails near campus like Canal Park. Lake Superior is super cool and in the summer it is a great place to hangout.

In the fall this town has amazing colors and it is amazing to watch as things change. The vibrant autumn foliage creates a stunning backdrop for outdoor activities, and the crisp air makes hiking and exploring even more enjoyable.

Overall it is a super cool area to live in and there is always things to do outside and new things that you are able to discover. From the Aerial Lift Bridge to the numerous hiking trails, Duluth offers endless opportunities for adventure and exploration.

The community in Duluth is also welcoming and friendly. Local businesses, restaurants, and events create a vibrant atmosphere that makes the city feel like home. Whether it's trying new restaurants downtown, attending local festivals, or simply enjoying a walk along the lakefront, there's always something happening in Duluth.

Campus Experience & Highlights

Some of my favorite highlights from college in Duluth so far would have to be meeting new people in the gym, pickleball courts, or at sporting events. I have enjoyed spending a lot of time outside and enjoying the warm weather we have had this year.

Campus Highlights

  • ▸ Recreation Facilities: The UMD Recreation and Outdoor Program (ROP) provides excellent facilities for staying active, including the gym, pickleball courts, and various intramural sports.
  • ▸ Academic Resources: The library and study spaces offer quiet, focused environments for academic work, while the business school provides specialized resources for finance students.
  • ▸ Student Organizations: There are numerous clubs and organizations that provide opportunities for networking, leadership development, and pursuing interests outside the classroom.
  • ▸ Campus Events: From sporting events to cultural activities, UMD hosts a variety of events that bring the campus community together and create memorable experiences.
  • ▸ Natural Setting: The campus itself is beautiful, with green spaces and proximity to natural areas that make it easy to enjoy the outdoors between classes.

Personal Experiences

One of my favorite aspects of campus life has been the opportunity to meet people from diverse backgrounds. Whether it's in the classroom, at the gym, or during campus events, I've made connections that have enriched my college experience.

The supportive environment at UMD has also been crucial to my success. Professors are accessible and willing to help, and the smaller class sizes mean I can get the attention I need to excel academically. This has been especially important as I balance my studies with my lawn care business and other commitments.

Iconic Duluth Landmarks

Aerial Lift Bridge

The iconic symbol of Duluth, this unique vertical-lift bridge connects Canal Park to Park Point and is one of the most photographed landmarks in Minnesota.

Enger Tower

Located in Enger Park, this five-story stone tower offers breathtaking panoramic views of Duluth, Lake Superior, and the surrounding area.

Glensheen Mansion

A historic 39-room estate built in the early 20th century, Glensheen provides a glimpse into Duluth's rich history and architectural heritage.

These iconic landmarks represent the beauty and history that make Duluth such a special place to live and study.
