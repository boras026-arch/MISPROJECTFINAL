My Hobbies & Interests

These activities shape who I am and bring balance to my life. Each hobby has taught me valuable lessons and created lasting memories.

Basketball

Basketball has been my favorite sport throughout my life, shaping me both as an athlete and a person. From my high school career to today's casual games, basketball continues to be my primary outlet for exercise and happiness.

Why Basketball Matters to Me

The sport has taught me invaluable lessons about leadership and team dynamics, effective communication, and personal discipline. Whether it's a competitive game or just shooting with friends, basketball remains my go-to activity for staying active and connected.

Memorable Experiences

  • ▸ Playing throughout high school taught me the importance of teamwork and dedication. The late-night practices and weekend tournaments created bonds with teammates that last to this day.
  • ▸ Today, I enjoy pickup games at the UMD rec center, where I've met many new friends and continue to stay active while having fun.
  • ▸ The competitive nature of basketball keeps me motivated and helps me maintain physical fitness while relieving stress from academic work.

Fishing

Fishing has grown from a casual activity into a beloved hobby that connects me with nature and loved ones. It's more than just catching fish—it's about finding peace in nature, quality time with family and friends, and developing patience and focus.

Why Fishing Matters to Me

Fishing has taught me that sometimes the best moments come from slowing down and appreciating the quiet. The early morning trips to local lakes provide a perfect escape from the busyness of college life.

Memorable Experiences

  • ▸ Some of my favorite memories are fishing trips with my family, where we spend hours on the water sharing stories and enjoying each other's company.
  • ▸ The challenge of learning different techniques and understanding fish behavior has made me more patient and observant in all aspects of life.
  • ▸ Fishing near Duluth has introduced me to beautiful spots along Lake Superior, combining my love of the outdoors with my new home in Minnesota.

Golf

Golf has become one of my favorite summer activities, combining technical challenge with peaceful outdoor enjoyment. The appeal of golf lies in technical mastery and continuous improvement, beautiful courses and outdoor scenery, and rewarding achievements.

Why Golf Matters to Me

Golf helps me focus and unwind. I enjoy the challenge, the strategy, and the time spent outdoors with friends and family. It's a sport that requires both mental and physical discipline.

Memorable Experiences

  • ▸ One of my most memorable golfing experiences was playing in Puerto Rico, where the course's beauty added an extra dimension to the game.
  • ▸ Golfing with my dad has created some of my favorite bonding moments, where we can talk about life while enjoying the game.
  • ▸ The satisfaction of improving my swing and seeing my scores drop over time has taught me the value of consistent practice and dedication.

Hunting

Hunting is a tradition in my family. It teaches patience, respect for nature, and provides a unique way to bond with loved ones. The experience of being in the wilderness, observing wildlife, and practicing responsible hunting has shaped my appreciation for the outdoors.

Why Hunting Matters to Me

Hunting connects me to family traditions and teaches me respect for nature and wildlife. It requires patience, skill, and ethical practices that translate to other areas of my life.

Memorable Experiences

  • ▸ Early morning hunts with my family have created some of my most cherished memories, where we share stories and enjoy the quiet of nature.
  • ▸ Learning about wildlife behavior and habitat has deepened my understanding of ecosystems and conservation.
  • ▸ The discipline required for hunting—from safety practices to ethical considerations—has taught me responsibility and respect.

Pickleball

Pickleball represents the perfect blend of competitive spirit and social enjoyment. This rapidly growing sport offers fast-paced, engaging gameplay, a great workout and active lifestyle, and social connections and new friendships.

Why Pickleball Matters to Me

The social aspect of pickleball makes it more than just a sport—it's a community. I've met many interesting people through pickleball, and it's become a fun way to stay active while building relationships.

Memorable Experiences

  • ▸ Playing pickleball at UMD has introduced me to a diverse group of students and faculty, creating connections across different departments and backgrounds.
  • ▸ The fast-paced nature of the game keeps me on my toes and provides an excellent cardiovascular workout.
  • ▸ Weekend pickleball tournaments have become a regular part of my routine, combining competition with socializing.
